Introducing RDY

Rendevouz & Disconnect Yourself

Just like a successful rendezvous, RDY brings people together by making event planning effortless. So why waste time stressing over scheduling when you can disconnect and let RDY do the work for you.

Sync your calendar and never miss a beat

Syncing your calendar is an important feature that allows you to access and update your schedule from multiple devices. With syncing, you can ensure that your calendar is up-to-date and accurate, even when you make changes on a different device. This feature also allows you to easily coordinate events with others, view shared calendars, and avoid scheduling conflicts. By syncing your calendar, you can save time and stay organized, ensuring that you never miss an important event or appointment.

Stay connected and stay coordinated

With the chat feature, you can easily discuss event details, share updates, and coordinate with other participants. The chat feature in RDY can also allow for file sharing, such as sharing photos, documents, or links, to make communication even more efficient. With the chat feature, you can stay connected and ensure that everyone is on the same page.
